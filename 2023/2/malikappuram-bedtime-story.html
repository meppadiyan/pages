Bedtime Story - Malikappuram

Bedtime story based on Malikappuram

Posted by admin on Mon, 27 Feb 2023

Once upon a time, there was a young girl named Kallu who loved her father more than anything else in the world. Kallu lived with her mother, father, and grandmother, and every night her grandmother would tell her brave stories of Lord Ayyappa, who was like a superhero to Kallu.

Kallu longed to go to Sabarimala, where Lord Ayyappa was said to live. She begged her father to take her there, but he couldn't go. One day, her father decided to take Kallu to Sabarimala, but unfortunately, he passed away.

Kallu was heartbroken, but her grandmother told her that her father was now with Lord Ayyappa. Kallu was comforted by this thought, and she decided to go to Sabarimala all by herself.

She was brave and determined, but on the way, some miscreants tried to kidnap her. Just when things seemed hopeless, a policeman appeared out of nowhere and saved Kallu from the rowdies. He then offered to help her reach Sabarimala.

Kallu was overjoyed and grateful for the policeman's help. Along with her cousin, she finally arrived at Sabarimala. Her cousin thought that Kallu had come all the way to Sabarimala because of her unfinished dream to visit Ayyappa, but Kallu had a different reason.

In a quiet moment at the temple, Kallu revealed to the police officer that she had come to ask Lord Ayyappa to take good care of her father. And with that, Kallu felt a sense of peace and closure that she had been longing for.

And so, Kallu and her cousin returned home with a new sense of purpose and a deeper appreciation for the strength and love that family can provide.
