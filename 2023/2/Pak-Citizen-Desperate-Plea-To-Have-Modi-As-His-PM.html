A Pakistan Citizen’s desperate plea to have Modi as his PM goes Viral.

“Modi much better for us. We don’t want Imran Khan...

Posted by admin on Fri, 24 Feb 2023
“We want Modi. We don’t want Imran Khan, Benazir , Nawaz Sharif or Mushraf. We just want Modi”
this Pak Citizen’s desperate plea amid worsening economic and security situation has gone viral.

Pakistan is facing the worst economic crisis in recent times. It has caused severe economic challenges for months due to which food, gas and oil prices have risen. And many are finding it extremely difficult to meet their ends. It’s at this point that the video shared by Pakistani YouTuber Sana Amjad went viral.

The video showed a fellow Pakistani praising PM Narendra Modi while venting his frustration at Pak PM Shehbaz Sharif Govt.

Only Narendra Modi can deal with all “mischievous elements” in Pak.
He added.

He also lauded the fact that India is now the fifth largest economy in the world.

“If partition hadn’t happened, we would have been in a much better position than we are in today”
He said while quoting the high prices for grocery now in Pakistan.
