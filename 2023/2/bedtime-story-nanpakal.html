Bedtime Story based on Nanpakal Nerathu Mayakkam

Spoilers Inside. Retelling of the same story from ...

Posted by self on Sat, 25 Feb 2023

Once upon a time, in a small village nestled in the mountains, lived a man named Sundar. Sundar lived with his parents. He had a loving wife and beautiful little daughter. They were a happy family. Sundar used to work hard to provide for his family, but, one day he went off to work and never returned.

Everyone in town assumed that Sundar was dead. His parents, wife and daughter mourned his loss and tried to move on with their lives, but, they could never forget him.

But, two years later, Sundar found himself in a bus on the outskirts of his village, with no memory of where he had been or what happened to him. For him, it felt like he woke up from a long nap.

He got out of the bus and walked back to his village. He then went on doing his chores. He changed his clothes, took a bath, went to the temple, had tea from the local shop, chit chatted with the crowd there. But, he felt something was off. Sundar noticed people behave a bit differently towards him.

After having breakfast from the village shop, he went to visit his barber for a haircut. There he saw himself in front of a mirror and it’s at that moment he realised that he is in someone else’s body.

Perhaps he woke up in some one else’s body who was passing through the village in that bus - He thought to himself.
He didn’t know how long this would last. Maybe until he goes to sleep again.

He realised that life is full of surprises and that he miss his family so much. What he considered mundane moments before now seem the most precious moments in his life.

He had a small nap in the afternoon. And when he woke up, he was no longer Sundar.
